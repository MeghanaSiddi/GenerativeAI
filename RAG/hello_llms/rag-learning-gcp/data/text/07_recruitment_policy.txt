# Recruitment and Hiring Policy

## Purpose
To ensure a fair, consistent, and legally compliant hiring process that attracts top talent.

## Equal Opportunity
- The company is an equal opportunity employer.
- Hiring decisions are based solely on qualifications, skills, and experience.
- Discrimination based on race, gender, age, religion, disability, or any protected characteristic is prohibited.

## Job Posting
- All open positions must be posted internally for at least 5 business days before external advertising.
- Job descriptions must accurately reflect the role requirements and responsibilities.

## Interview Process
- A minimum of 2 interviews are required for all permanent positions.
- Interview panels must include at least one HR representative.
- Structured interview questions must be used to ensure consistency.

## Background Checks
- All final candidates are subject to background verification including employment history and education.
- Criminal background checks are conducted where legally permitted and role-relevant.

## Offer and Onboarding
- Verbal offers must be followed by a written offer letter within 2 business days.
- New hires must complete onboarding within their first week, including policy acknowledgment.

## Employee Referrals
- Employees may refer candidates for open positions.
- A referral bonus of $1,000 is paid after the referred employee completes 6 months of service.
