# Anti-Harassment and Workplace Respect Policy

## Purpose
The company is committed to providing a workplace free from harassment, bullying, and discrimination.

## Definition of Harassment
Harassment includes any unwanted conduct that creates a hostile, intimidating, or offensive work environment, including:
- Verbal abuse, insults, or offensive jokes
- Unwanted physical contact
- Sexual harassment or inappropriate advances
- Cyberbullying or online harassment
- Intimidation or threats

## Reporting Harassment
- Employees who experience or witness harassment should report it to HR or their manager immediately.
- Reports can be made anonymously through the company's ethics hotline.
- All complaints will be investigated promptly and confidentially.

## Investigation Process
- HR will acknowledge the complaint within 2 business days.
- An impartial investigation will be conducted within 15 business days.
- Both parties will be informed of the outcome.

## Consequences
- Substantiated harassment complaints will result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination.
- Retaliation against anyone who reports harassment in good faith is strictly prohibited.
