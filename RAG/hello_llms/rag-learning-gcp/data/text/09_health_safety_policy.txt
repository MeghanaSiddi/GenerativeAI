# Health and Safety Policy

## Purpose
The company is committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment for all employees, contractors, and visitors.

## Responsibilities
- Management: Ensure all workplaces comply with health and safety regulations and provide necessary resources.
- Employees: Follow all safety procedures, report hazards, and participate in safety training.
- HR: Maintain safety records, coordinate training, and manage incident reporting.

## Workplace Safety Rules
- Emergency exits must remain clear and unobstructed at all times.
- Employees must report any unsafe conditions or equipment to their manager immediately.
- Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) must be worn where required.
- No employee should operate equipment they are not trained to use.

## Incident Reporting
- All workplace accidents, injuries, or near-misses must be reported to HR within 24 hours.
- An incident report form must be completed and submitted to the safety officer.
- Serious incidents will be investigated within 48 hours.

## Emergency Procedures
- Fire evacuation drills are conducted twice a year.
- Emergency contact numbers are posted at all workstations and common areas.
- First aid kits are available on every floor and employees are encouraged to get first aid certified.

## Mental Health
- The company provides access to an Employee Assistance Program (EAP) for mental health support.
- Mental health days are included within the sick leave entitlement.
- Managers are trained to recognize signs of stress and burnout.
