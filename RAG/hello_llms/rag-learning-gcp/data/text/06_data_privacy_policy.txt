# Data Privacy and Confidentiality Policy

## Purpose
To protect sensitive company and employee information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

## Confidential Information
Confidential information includes but is not limited to:
- Employee personal data (salary, health records, contact details)
- Client and customer data
- Business strategies, financial data, and trade secrets
- Proprietary software and intellectual property

## Employee Obligations
- Employees must not share confidential information with unauthorized parties.
- Confidential documents must be stored securely and not left unattended.
- Employees must sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) upon joining.
- Confidentiality obligations continue after employment ends.

## Data Handling
- Personal data must be collected and processed in accordance with applicable privacy laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).
- Data should only be accessed on a need-to-know basis.
- Data breaches must be reported to the IT Security team within 1 hour of discovery.

## Data Retention
- Employee records are retained for 7 years after termination.
- Customer data is retained only as long as necessary for business purposes.

## Violations
Unauthorized disclosure of confidential information may result in disciplinary action and legal proceedings.
