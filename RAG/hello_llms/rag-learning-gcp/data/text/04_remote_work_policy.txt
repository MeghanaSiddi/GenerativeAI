# Remote Work Policy

## Eligibility
- Employees in roles that can be performed remotely may apply for remote work arrangements.
- Eligibility is determined by the department head in consultation with HR.

## Work Hours
- Remote employees must be available during core hours: 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM local time.
- Employees are expected to maintain the same productivity standards as in-office work.

## Equipment and Security
- The company will provide a laptop and necessary software for remote work.
- Employees must use a secure, password-protected internet connection (VPN required).
- Company data must not be stored on personal devices.

## Communication
- Remote employees must attend all scheduled team meetings via video call.
- Response to messages and emails is expected within 2 hours during working hours.
- Employees must update their availability status on the company communication platform.

## Home Office Requirements
- Employees are responsible for maintaining a safe and ergonomic home workspace.
- The company may provide a one-time home office stipend of up to $500.

## Performance Review
- Remote work arrangements will be reviewed every 6 months.
- Arrangements may be revoked if performance or communication standards are not met.
