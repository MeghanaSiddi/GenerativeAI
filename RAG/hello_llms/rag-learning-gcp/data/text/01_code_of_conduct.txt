# Code of Conduct Policy

## Purpose
This policy establishes the standards of professional behavior expected from all employees.

## Standards of Conduct
- Treat all colleagues, clients, and partners with respect and professionalism.
- Maintain honesty and integrity in all business dealings.
- Avoid conflicts of interest and disclose any potential conflicts to HR immediately.
- Do not engage in any form of fraud, theft, or dishonest behavior.
- Represent the company positively in all internal and external communications.

## Use of Company Resources
- Company resources (equipment, software, internet) must be used for business purposes only.
- Personal use of company assets should be minimal and must not interfere with work duties.

## Social Media
- Employees must not post confidential company information on social media.
- Public statements about the company must be approved by the communications team.

## Violations
Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.
