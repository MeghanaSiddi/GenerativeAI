# Compensation and Benefits Policy

## Salary Structure
- Salaries are determined based on role, experience, market benchmarks, and internal equity.
- Salary bands are reviewed annually and adjusted based on market data.
- Employees will receive a written compensation statement at the time of hire and upon any changes.

## Pay Schedule
- Employees are paid on the last business day of each month via direct bank transfer.
- Payslips are issued electronically through the HR portal.

## Annual Salary Review
- Salary reviews are conducted annually in January based on performance ratings and budget availability.
- Merit increases are not guaranteed and are subject to company financial performance.

## Bonuses
- Performance bonuses are awarded based on individual and company performance.
- Bonus eligibility requires a minimum rating of "Meets Expectations" in the annual review.
- Bonuses are paid in February following the review cycle.

## Benefits
- Health Insurance: Company covers 80% of premium for employee and 50% for dependents.
- Retirement Plan: Company matches employee contributions up to 5% of base salary.
- Life Insurance: Coverage equal to 2x annual salary provided at no cost to the employee.
- Wellness Allowance: $300 per year for gym memberships or wellness activities.
- Learning & Development: Up to $2,000 per year for approved training and certifications.

## Overtime
- Non-exempt employees are eligible for overtime pay at 1.5x their hourly rate for hours exceeding 40 per week.
- Overtime must be pre-approved by the department manager.
