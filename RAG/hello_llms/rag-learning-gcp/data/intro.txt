# Introduction to HR Policies

## Welcome

Welcome to our organization! We are committed to creating a professional, fair, safe, and respectful working environment for everyone. As a company that works with gig workers, freelancers, and independent contractors, we recognize the importance of flexibility while maintaining high standards of ethics, quality, and collaboration.

Our Human Resources (HR) policies provide clear guidelines about how we work together, what you can expect from us, and what we expect from you. These policies are designed to ensure consistency, transparency, legal compliance, and mutual respect.

Whether you are joining us for a short-term project or working with us regularly, these policies will help you understand your rights, responsibilities, and the standards of conduct expected while representing our organization.

Our HR policies cover topics such as:

* Code of Conduct
* Equal Opportunity and Non-Discrimination
* Anti-Harassment and Workplace Respect
* Confidentiality and Data Protection
* Information Security
* Working Hours and Availability
* Payments and Invoicing
* Performance Expectations
* Communication Guidelines
* Conflict of Interest
* Health and Safety
* Leave and Time-Off (where applicable)
* Grievance Resolution
* Disciplinary Process
* Termination of Engagement
* Compliance with Laws and Regulations

These policies may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in business needs, industry standards, or applicable laws. Workers are encouraged to review the latest version whenever updates are announced.

If you have any questions regarding these policies, please reach out to the HR team or your designated engagement manager.

By accepting an assignment or continuing your engagement with the organization, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to comply with these HR policies.

Together, we can build a workplace that values professionalism, integrity, collaboration, and trust while preserving the flexibility that makes gig work successful.
